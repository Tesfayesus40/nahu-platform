# Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading digital agricultural ecosystem that connects every participant in the agricultural value chain through one intelligent platform.

---

# Mission

To empower Ethiopian agriculture by providing a secure, transparent, AI-powered digital marketplace where agricultural products can be traded efficiently while supporting farmers, cooperatives, exporters, government institutions, logistics providers, and financial services.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Build the digital infrastructure that enables Ethiopia's agricultural economy to operate more efficiently, transparently, and globally.

---

# Platform Principles

- Farmer First
- Buyer Friendly
- Secure by Design
- AI Powered
- Mobile First
- Offline First
- Multi-language Support
- Scalable
- Transparent
