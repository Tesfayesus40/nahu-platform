# Nahu Platform

# Business Actors

Version: 1.0

---

## Purpose

This document defines every actor that can interact with the Nahu Platform.

These actors form the foundation of the Identity and Access Management (IAM) system.

---

# Business Actors

| Category | Actor | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Producer | Farmer | Produces agricultural products |
| Producer | Cooperative | Represents groups of farmers |
| Producer | Union | Represents multiple cooperatives |
| Business | Local Buyer | Purchases products within Ethiopia |
| Business | International Buyer | Purchases products from outside Ethiopia |
| Business | Exporter | Exports products internationally |
| Business | Processor | Processes agricultural products |
| Business | Retailer | Sells products to consumers |
| Logistics | Transport Company | Provides transportation services |
| Logistics | Warehouse | Stores products |
| Logistics | Cold Storage | Stores temperature-sensitive products |
| Finance | Bank | Provides payment services |
| Finance | Microfinance Institution | Provides agricultural financing |
| Finance | Insurance Company | Provides agricultural insurance |
| Government | Ministry of Agriculture | Policy and oversight |
| Government | Ethiopian Coffee and Tea Authority | Coffee regulation |
| Government | Ethiopian Customs Commission | Export and import control |
| Inspection | Quality Inspector | Inspects agricultural products |
| Platform | Administrator | Full platform administration |
| Platform | Customer Support | Assists platform users |
| Platform | AI Agent | Provides intelligent assistance |
